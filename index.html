Research

Formation of Extremal Black Holes in Spherically Symmetric Einstein–Maxwell Charged Scalar Field System.

Mentored by Professor Christoph Kehle. I am studying the formation of extremal (zero surface temperature) black holes in the spherically symmetric Einstein–Maxwell charged scalar field system. The project aims to extend recent work on collapse to extremal Reissner–Nordström black holes by removing the coupling constant–mass requirement $|\mathfrak{e}M| \gg 1$ and proving that extremal formation can occur even for $|\mathfrak{e}M| \ll 1$.
